Technical Sizing Information

Why do sizes vary?

Each manufacturer uses different manufacturing lasts. A Nike size 42 might measure 26.5 cm, while an ASICS size 42 measures 26.5 cm or 25.25 cm depending on the model. The only universal and reliable measurement is the internal length.

What is the Optimal Fit?

If your exact length does not match the scale of the target brand, the calculator will select the next size up (usually +0.25 cm or +0.5 cm). This ensures that your foot has the proper clearance to avoid friction and discomfort.

How to measure your foot

Stand on a piece of paper with your heel against the wall. Trace the outline of your longest toe. Measure the distance. It is advisable to add about 0.5 cm (0.2 in) of extra margin for running shoes.
